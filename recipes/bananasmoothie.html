how to make a banana smoothie

Description

This recipe will yeild the best banana smoothie you can try, fresh and fit for any season

Ingrediets

  • 2-3 bananas
  • 300 ml milk
  • 20 grams sugar
  • 60 ml condenced milk
  • ice

Steps

  1. peel your banans and place them into the blender
  2. fill it up with your milk and condensed milk
  3. blend for 5 minutes
  4. add sugar and blend for another 1 minute
  5. serve cold with ice
