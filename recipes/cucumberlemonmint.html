How to make a refrehsing cucumber smoothie

Description

This refreshing beverage is perfect to quench your summertiem thirst

you need a blender to make this recipe

Ingrediets

  • 2 cucumbers
  • 1 lemon juice freshly squezed
  • a few mint leaves
  • 100 ml of water
  • 20 grams of sugar

Steps

  1. Peel your cucumbers
  2. put all ingrediets into blender and blend away
