How to cook lasagna

Description

How to cook lasagna, a full guide for beginers to make tastey lasagna for your food cravings

You will need meat, lasagna sheets, tomatoe paste and a tray to fill it up. A preheated oven to ensure proper cooking

Ingrediets

  • Lasagna store bought sheets
  • Tomatoe sauce
  • Meat thingy
  • Optional:Basil

Steps

  1. Soak the pasta sheets and place 1 as the base
  2. add the sauce
  3. add the meat
  4. add another layer of pasta sheet
  5. repeat until you have desired thickness
